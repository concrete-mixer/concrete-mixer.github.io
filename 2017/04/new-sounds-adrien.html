New sounds by Adrien Capozzi

April 1, 2017

In my quest to get contributions to Concrete Mixer I’ve turned to muso friends with the view that, well, everyone who has dabbled in electronica has made field recordings, right? Well my LA-based chum Adrien Capozzi said he hadn’t, but that he’d make some, and a few days later emailed me two tracks. That’s exactly the sort of spirit I’m after! Well done that man.

Contributions

Chimes Cambria

Adrien didn’t provide any description of what either recording is about, so I don’t have much to go on except the titles. When I google ‘Chimes Cambria’ this is the result I get, but that doesn’t seem likely. The good thing about sound is you can enjoy it without knowing what it is, and this recording is just the ticket.

Underwood Family Farm

Again there’s no context to this recording other than the title. My knowledge of American farming being fairly limited, I’m tempted to imagine this recording was made during a barn raising. (I guess this isn’t very likely.) No matter what the occasion, the recording with sounds of children at gentle(?) play sounds dreamily relaxed.

Thanks Adrien for adding your ingredients to Concrete Mixer’s sonic gumbo!
